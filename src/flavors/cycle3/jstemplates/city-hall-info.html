{{#_}}City-wide Ideas{{/_}}

{{#_}}City-wide ideas apply to all of Boston, not a specific location or neighborhood.{{/_}}

{{#_}}On the map, city-wide ideas appear in a ring around City Hall. You can click on them just like any other idea, or filter and read them-by-one using the sidebar or the scope dropdown in list view.{{/_}}

{{#_}}To share your own city-wide idea, click on "Share an idea", then click on the "City-wide" button.{{/_}}
